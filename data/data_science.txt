DATA SCIENCE

Introduction

Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that combines statistics, mathematics, computer science, machine learning, data analysis, and domain expertise to extract meaningful insights from data. Organizations use data science to make informed decisions, predict future trends, optimize operations, and gain competitive advantages.

In today's digital world, massive amounts of data are generated every second from websites, social media platforms, mobile applications, sensors, financial transactions, healthcare systems, and business operations. Data Science helps transform this raw data into valuable knowledge.

History of Data Science

The foundations of Data Science come from statistics, mathematics, and computer science. The term Data Science became widely popular in the 21st century as organizations began collecting and processing massive datasets.

The growth of big data, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and machine learning has significantly increased the importance of data science across industries.
